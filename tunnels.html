Tunnels

This work is part of the course 'Taller de Diseño Multimedial 3', done with my group 'El Cuarteto Multimedial', in which we had to simulate an experience about a myth of the city where I live. We chose to work with tunnels and, through our own modeling and the use of assets, we built a simulation of an experience about trying to find someone who was kidnapped and asked for help through the tunnels that run underground throughout the city.
