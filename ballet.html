Ballet

This was a project for University for the subject 3D Multimedia Animation. We had to precisely model a real object, and I chose a dancer from a music box.

The process of 3D modeling the ballerina from the music box was quite a challenge that I thoroughly enjoyed undertaking. Every detail, from the graceful movements to the folds of the dress, required precision and care to capture the essence of dance. Seeing the final result was incredibly rewarding; every hour of work was worth it. I'm thrilled to have been able to complete this project and I'm eager to share it with everyone. It's an experience I'll cherish forever!
